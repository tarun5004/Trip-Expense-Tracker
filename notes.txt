Key Architecture Decisions
Decoupled via EventEmitter — services call emitRealtimeEvent() and never import socket.io. The realtime layer can be disabled entirely without touching business logic.
DB-verified room joins — every group:join checks groupModel.isMember() from the database. Never trust client claims.
Idempotency — every S→C payload includes a UUID eventId. The client dedup set (capped at 1000) drops replays from reconnection recovery.
Dual room architecture — group:{id} for group-scoped events, user:{id} for personal notifications.
Service integration complete — expense.service.js and payment.service.js emit realtime events after every create/update/delete mutation + a paired balance:updated event.
Graceful shutdown — closeSocketServer() added to server.js shutdown sequence.
